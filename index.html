Alumni Networking

Find and connect with batchmates, form interest groups (region, department, year) and expand your professional circle. Use the directory to search by batch, skills or location.

  • Search & Filter: find alumni by batch, city or expertise.
  • Create Groups: start cohort groups for events, reunions or project collaborations.
  • Private Messaging: contact alumni securely (opt-in feature).

Mentoring

Experienced alumni offer guidance, mentorship and career advice to students and fresh grads. Sign up as a mentor or request a mentor based on skillset and availability.

  • Mentor Match: match by industry, role or skills.
  • Short-term Guidance: 1:1 sessions, resume reviews & mock interviews.
  • Long-term Mentorship: ongoing career coaching and goal planning.

Career Updates

Access hand-picked job openings, internships and skill-building programs shared by alumni and partner organisations. Employers can post opportunities for alumni and current students.

  • Curated Jobs: roles posted by alumni with verified experience.
  • Internships & Projects: short-term internships and capstone opportunities.
  • Events & Workshops: career fairs, upskilling webinars and hackathons.

Why join?

  • Strengthen your professional network
  • Give or receive mentorship
  • Exclusive career updates
